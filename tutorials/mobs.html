The Busher

Is it a bush? Yes. Is it bushy? No.

The Busher was born in v0.6; inspired by @cheerfullbull's Tree Monster, the Busher will spawn out of nowhere and is a melee mob that moves towards you and has a cooldown time of 0.2 seconds. Killing it will give you 1-9 wood and 0-2 apples.
