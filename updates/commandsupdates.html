/takedamage Trails

In v0.3.15, the command /takedamage added an interesting exploit.

Sure, while /kill is shorter, there is an interesting trick you can pull with /takedamage: /takedamage trails.

How is works is, you open up the chat and type /takedamage. Nothing out of the ordinary, right?

What this does is kill you immediately. But the blood particles will continue to fall from your body even if you respawn because of the immense damage you took. Therefore, you have this red trail wherever you go.
