The Busher

The Busher, the ambusher. Nevermind.

v0.6 was the version where the Busher was added. It was inspired by @cheerfullbull's Tree Monster. It is a bush with apples as eyes and an angry expression, and killing it gives you 9 wood and 1 apple.
